Team management lives under **Team** in the sidebar. From here you invite teammates, change their roles, and organise them into crews.

Only owners can access the Team and Crews pages. Office and tech members see a "no access" notice if they navigate there directly.

## Roles

Zoop has three roles. Every person you invite gets exactly one.

| Role | What they can do |
|------|-----------------|
| **Owner** | Full access — billing, integrations, team management, and everything below. |
| **Office** | Operational access — schedule, customers, jobs. No financials, no settings. |
| **Tech** | Own assigned jobs only. No customer list, no settings. |

The invite form defaults to **Tech** (least privilege). Change the role before sending if the person needs broader access.

<Callout type="warning">
Every tenant must have at least one owner at all times. Zoop prevents you from demoting or removing the last remaining owner.
</Callout>

## Invite a teammate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team settings">
    Select **Team** in the sidebar. Scroll to the **Invite a new member** section at the bottom of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their email">
    Type the person's email address. If they already have a Zoop account, you'll see a note: "Existing Zoop user — they'll skip account creation."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a role">
    Pick **Owner**, **Office**, or **Tech**. A short description under the dropdown explains what each role can do.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invite">
    Select **Send invite**. Zoop emails them a link to accept and set up their account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The invite appears in the **Pending invites** section above the member list until the person accepts.

---

## Manage pending invites

Pending invites show the recipient's email, the role they were invited as, who sent the invite, when it was sent, and when it expires.

From the pending-invites table you can:

- **Resend** — send the invite email again (useful if it landed in spam or the link expired). The expiry resets when you resend.
- **Revoke** — cancel the invite. The link in the email stops working immediately.

## Change a member's role

Find the person in the **Members** table and use the role dropdown in their row. The change takes effect immediately — no confirmation dialog. If something goes wrong, Zoop shows an error toast and restores the previous role in the table.

You cannot change your own role if you are the only owner of the account.

---

## Remove a member

Select the remove icon (the circle-with-slash) on the member's row and confirm. The person loses access immediately. You can invite them back at any time.

You cannot remove the last owner of the account.

## Crews

A crew is a named group of teammates. When you dispatch a job to a crew, all members of that crew are assigned to it — no need to add people one by one.

Crews are managed by selecting **Team** in the sidebar, then **Crews** from the submenu.

### Create a crew

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Crews">
    Select **Team** in the sidebar, then **Crews** from the submenu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select New crew">
    Select the **New crew** button in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name the crew">
    Give it a short name your team will recognise — for example, "Tuesday crew" or "Henderson & Mike". Select **Create**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Add or remove crew members

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open manage members">
    On the crew's row, select the people icon. A dialog opens showing current members and available teammates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a teammate">
    In the **Available teammates** section, select the plus icon next to the person's name. They appear in the **In this crew** list immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove a teammate">
    In the **In this crew** section, select the minus icon next to the person's name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close the dialog">
    Select **Done** when finished. Changes are saved as you go — there is no separate save step.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Rename a crew

On the crew's row, select the pencil icon, edit the name, and select **Save**. The new name appears on any future jobs. Jobs that ran before the rename keep the old name on their history.

### Archive a crew

Archiving hides a crew from the dispatch picker without deleting its history. Select the circle-with-slash icon on the crew's row and confirm. Archived jobs that already used the crew retain the crew name.

To see archived crews, turn on the **Show archived** toggle above the table.

### Unarchive a crew

With **Show archived** enabled, find the crew in the table and select the undo icon. The crew becomes available in the dispatch picker again.

---

## Dispatch a crew to a job

Crew assignment happens from the [dispatch board](/using-zoop/dispatch) or from the job detail page, not from Settings. Once you have created a crew and added members, the crew name appears in the assignee picker when you schedule or edit a job.
