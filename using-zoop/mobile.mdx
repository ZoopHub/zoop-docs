The Zoop mobile app puts your daily work in your pocket: today's jobs, your full job list, customers, invoices, quotes, and a quick-capture button that follows you between screens. You sign in with the same credentials you use on the web.

## Sign in

Open the app and sign in with your email and password, or tap **Continue with Google** or **Continue with Apple**. If you manage more than one business in Zoop, you'll see a business picker after signing in — tap the one you want to work with.

## Bottom navigation

Five tabs sit at the bottom of the screen. Which tabs you see depends on your role.

| Tab | Who sees it |
|---|---|
| Today | Everyone |
| Jobs | Everyone |
| Customers | Owners and office managers |
| Invoices | Everyone |
| More | Everyone |

Tech users who don't have the Customers tab see **Quotes** in its place.

The **+** button (floating above the tab bar) opens the note composer from any screen.

## Today

The Today screen shows every job scheduled for the current calendar day, sorted by start time. Each row shows the job title, customer name, scheduled time, and a colour-coded status badge.

Job statuses in the app:

- **Scheduled** — confirmed and on the calendar
- **Unscheduled** — created but not yet given a time
- **Done** — work completed
- **Cancelled** — job was called off

Tap any job to open its detail. If there are no jobs today, the screen says so — nothing is hidden.

## Jobs

The Jobs screen lists all jobs across all dates. From here you can find a specific job, filter by status, or sort the list to match how you work.

**To find a job:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search">
    Type a customer name or job title into the search field at the top of the screen. The list narrows as you type.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by status">
    Tap a status chip — All, Scheduled, Unscheduled, Done, or Cancelled — to show only jobs in that state. Tap the active chip again (or tap All) to clear it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sort">
    Use the Sort picker to order by time, status, or customer name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open">
    Tap any job row to open the full detail.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The header shows how many jobs match your current filter versus your total count (for example, "3 of 14 jobs"). Tap **Refresh** to pull the latest data from the server without leaving the screen.

## Customers

The Customers screen lists every customer in your account. It is visible to owners and office managers only — field techs see Quotes in this tab position instead.

**To find a customer:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search">
    Type a name or phone number into the search field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sort">
    Switch the sort between alphabetical (by name) or by job count, so your busiest accounts float to the top.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open">
    Tap a customer row to open their full record.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each row shows the customer's name, phone number, email, total job count, and an **Active** badge if they currently have at least one scheduled job.

## Invoices

The Invoices screen gives you a live view of your money. When you have an outstanding balance or collected payments this month, summary cards appear at the top showing those amounts.

**To find an invoice:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search">
    Type an invoice number or customer name into the search field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by status">
    Tap a status chip — All, Sent, Paid, Overdue, Draft, or Cancelled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sort">
    Order by date, amount, customer name, or status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open">
    Tap any row to open the invoice detail.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each row shows the invoice number, customer name, issue date, total amount, and status badge.

## Quotes

The Quotes screen lists every quote in your account. If any quotes are in **Accepted** status, a banner at the top tells you how many are ready to convert to invoices.

**To find a quote:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search">
    Type a quote title or customer name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by status">
    Tap a status chip — All, Sent, Accepted, Draft, Declined, or Expired.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sort">
    Order by date, amount, customer name, or status.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open">
    Tap any row to open the quote detail.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Each row shows the quote title, customer name, creation date, total (sum of line items), and status badge.

## Quick capture (the + button)

The **+** button is always visible above the tab bar. Tap it to open the note composer — a fast way to log anything while your hands are dirty or you're mid-job.

### Type a note

Type directly into the text area and tap **Save**. The note is saved against your account and synced to the web.

### Record a voice note

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap Record note">
    The app asks for microphone permission the first time. Grant it to continue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Speak">
    A timer shows how long you've been recording.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Stop">
    Tap the Stop button (it shows the elapsed seconds while recording). The audio is uploaded and transcribed automatically. The transcribed text appears in the text area.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and save">
    Review the transcript, make any corrections, then tap **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If transcription fails, you have three options:

- **Try again** — retry the transcription with the same audio
- **Skip transcription** — discard the audio and return to the text field so you can type manually
- **Discard** — throw away the recording and start over
