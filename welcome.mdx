Zoop is built for solo contractors and small teams: plumbers, electricians, HVAC techs, landscapers, handymen. If you run a business from a truck, Zoop handles the paperwork so you can focus on the work.

This page gives you a quick map of how Zoop is laid out and where to start.

## How Zoop is laid out

Every job follows the same five steps. The bold labels below match the sections in Zoop's left-hand menu — that's exactly where you'll go for each one.

**Customers** — All your customer details, job history, and notes in one place. No more digging through texts and emails to remember what you quoted someone last spring.

**Quotes** — Send a professional quote from your phone in under two minutes. Your customer gets a link to approve it with one tap. When they approve it, Zoop converts it to a job automatically — nothing to copy over.

**Jobs** — Track every job from scheduled to complete. Assign it to a tech, log notes and photos on-site, and add parts and labor as you go.

**Invoices** — When the job is done, the invoice is already built from the job. Send it by SMS or email and your customer pays through a secure link. No chasing checks.

**Payments** — Money goes straight to your account. You can see what's come in, what's outstanding, and what's overdue at a glance.
