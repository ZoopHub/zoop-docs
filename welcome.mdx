Zoop is built for solo contractors and small teams: plumbers, electricians, HVAC techs, landscapers, handymen. Zoop covers the whole job — from the first customer call to the money in your account.

The core flow is simple: add a customer, send a quote, run the job, send the invoice, get paid. Everything connects, so nothing falls through the cracks.

## How Zoop works

Every job follows the same path.

**Customer** — All your customer details, job history, and notes in one place. No more digging through texts and emails to remember what you quoted someone last spring.

**Quote** — Send a professional quote from your phone in under two minutes. Your customer gets a link to approve it with one tap. When they do, it converts to a job automatically.

**Job** — Track every job from scheduled to complete. Assign it to a tech, log notes and photos on-site, and log the parts and labor as you go.

**Invoice** — When the job is done, the invoice is ready. Send it by SMS or email — your customer pays through a secure link. No chasing checks.

**Get paid** — Payments go straight to your account. You see exactly what's come in, what's outstanding, and what's overdue.
